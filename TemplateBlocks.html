Welcome to Intervest

We take pride in engineering enterprise software solutions and providing dedicated support for businesses. We specialize in a multitude of technologies including Java EE and Oracle. If you would like to join our development centre, we would like to hear from you.


We are...

Intervest Software Technologies (Private) Limited is a Board of Investment approved information technology and related services provider incorporated in 2007 by a British investor and entrepreneur. The company develops enterprise class web applications predominantly based on the JavaEE technology stack. These systems power one of UK s’ largest insurance brokers: www.staysure.co.uk. The products range from front-end web applications to mission critical back- office systems used by Staysure callcentre. A growing team of seventy young professionals who are the products of leading technology schools of Sri Lanka are permanently employed at present including system support, quality testing and UI/UX design teams.
